About Me

My name is Harold Talavera. I come from the Philippines, a country rich in culture and warmth. I worked for 12 years as a C# ASP.Net Developer in a media company.

I love to travel, which is why it's the theme of my website. I have already visited South Korea, Tokyo Japan, Hong Kong, Singapore, and, especially, the beautiful beaches in my home country, the Philippines.

These travels have been more than just sightseeing. They've been a journey of self-discovery, allowing me to immerse myself in diverse cultures and breathtaking landscapes. Each experience has not only broadened my perspectives but has also provided me with valuable insights.
